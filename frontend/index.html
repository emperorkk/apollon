Disclaimer & Legal Notice

Apollon is an independent news aggregation and intelligence dashboard. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any of the news organisations whose content appears here.

Source material

Every headline, summary, and article record shown here originates from a publicly available RSS feed that the publisher itself voluntarily makes available for syndication. Apollon does not scrape, bypass paywalls, or access any non-public content. Every article links directly back to its original publisher, and readers are encouraged to visit the source for the full story.

Summaries

Article summaries are independently generated by an AI model from a short excerpt of the original text — they are not verbatim reproductions or extracts of the original articles.

Copyright basis (EU)

Directive (EU) 2019/790 (the "DSM Directive"), Article 15, grants press publishers rights over reproduction of press publications by online services, but explicitly excludes hyperlinking and the use of individual words or "very short extracts" (Art. 15(1), 15(3)). Per the Court of Justice of the EU (Svensson, C-466/12; GS Media, C-160/15), hyperlinking to content a rightsholder has made freely and lawfully available online does not itself constitute a "communication to the public" requiring separate authorisation. Article 15(3) also preserves the existing quotation and other exceptions under Directive 2001/29/EC.

Rights holders with concerns about how their content is represented here can contact and the article in question will be reviewed promptly.

Privacy & IP logging

Apollon uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to measure real-user page-load performance (Real User Measurements). It is a cookie-free, privacy-first service — it does not set cookies, does not use localStorage, and does not fingerprint visitors via IP address, user agent, or any other means; it only aggregates anonymised performance metrics and is not used to track or profile individuals. It is configured to exclude visitor data from the EU.

Apollon does not use advertising trackers or build profiles of visitors. The only other thing logged about a visit is the IP address used to reach the service, used exclusively for automated bot protection (rate-limiting and firewall rules).
